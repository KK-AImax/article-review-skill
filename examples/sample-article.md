# How To Choose A Team Knowledge Base

In this article, we will discuss why choosing a knowledge base is important for modern teams. A good tool can be a game-changer because it helps everyone find information. There are many options, and each company should think carefully before deciding.

## Features

Teams should look at search, permissions, integrations, templates, and analytics. Learn more at [learn more](https://example.com).

## Pricing

Pricing is important because teams need a tool that fits their budget. The finance team should review the final contract.

## Security

Confidential roadmap notes should be stored carefully. Internal use only: the migration plan depends on unreleased supplier terms.
